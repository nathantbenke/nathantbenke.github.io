About Me

Here's my story :)

Hey there, I’m Nathan, a 24-year-old creative technologist with a passion for building the future of XR. I hold a Master of Science (MSc) in Virtual and Augmented Reality from Goldsmiths, University of London, and a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science with a minor certificate in 4D Digital Art from the University of Wisconsin-Madison. My journey so far has been all about blending technical expertise with artistic vision, which even led my team to win at the Innovate UK Immersive Tech Awards.

I’ve also had the opportunity to work with industry leaders like Sandia National Laboratories, where I contributed the creation of digital twin visualizations in Unity for multidisciplinary models as well as researching methods for streamlining workflows for implementing complex Unity visualizations. At 3D Systems, I dove into prototyping and industrial/medical 3D printing applications, creating interactive AR instruction tools for healthcare and industrial end users and the support staff. During these internships, I honed my ability to collaborate with cross-functional teams of subject matter experts and developers.

I love crafting immersive, cutting-edge XR experiences, blending technical skill with creative flair. When I’m not developing in Unity, I enjoy diving into music composition and production in Ableton. Alternatively, you might find me traveling abroad, trying to find new inspiration from the diverse cultures and experiences I come across. These experiences have helped me find new ways to merge the digital and physical worlds. For my master’s thesis, The Exploration of Real-Time Graphics Remapping, I worked extensively with RealSense depth sensor camera data streams to investigate how real-world objects could be retextured and manipulated in real-time, pushing the boundaries of augmented reality interaction.

I’m always excited to experiment with emerging technologies, stay ahead of tech trends, and bring groundbreaking ideas to life. Eligible to work in the US, as well as the UK under a graduate visa, and I’m ready to start immediately!

Goldsmiths, University of London

September 2024

Graduated with a Master of Science in Virtual and Augmented Reality. Following the research pathway, my master’s thesis utilized coursework in computer graphics and 3D mathematics to explore the use of depth sensor camera data streams for graphically remapping reality in Unity, employing shader computation, visual effects graphs, and object reprojection (Vuforia). The goal was to alter real-world objects in an immersive environment in real time. With further development, this approach has potential applications across numerous industries, including gaming, entertainment, art, and training.
The MSc program provided me with a strong foundation in both the technical and creative aspects of XR development, preparing me to work in a diverse range of fields by understanding how immersive technologies can be leveraged to solve real-world challenges, create engaging experiences, and enhance user interaction in various professional contexts.
